Hi! I'm Zac, a fourth-year Computer Science student at The University of Nottingham. I'm interested in programming languages (mostly functional ones) and how they can be made easier to use (e.g. interactive programming, clever type systems), plus things like program synthesis, holes, hole-filling, and anything else to do with incomplete programs. I'm also really interested in type theory. I'm pretty sure, in the future, dependently typed, interactive, functional programming languages will be the norm—but for the time being I will use Haskell for everything, instead.

I'm currently the (outgoing) President of HackSoc. I used to be the Development Secretary. I was an organiser of HackNotts 2021, our annual hackathon (which actually happened in 2022,) and the lead of last year's HackNotts '23.

Most of my old projects are completely unfinished, but feel free to have a look around my GitHub page nevertheless. There's some cool stuff there!
